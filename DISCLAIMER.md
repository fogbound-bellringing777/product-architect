## NOT Professional Advice

**This repository is an educational and operational reference framework.
It is NOT a substitute for professional advice in any domain.**

### Legal & Compliance

The policies, compliance frameworks, and legal templates in this repository
(Agent 10, Agent 11, country compliance deep-dives) are **starting points
for discussion with qualified legal professionals, not final legal documents.**

Specifically:
- **All 14 policy drafts** in Agent 11 (Compliance & Ethics) MUST be reviewed
  and customized by a qualified lawyer before adoption. They are frameworks,
  not jurisdiction-specific legal instruments.
- **Country compliance deep-dives** (India, US, EU, UK, SEA) reflect regulations
  as of early 2026 and may be outdated. Laws change. Always verify with local
  legal counsel.
- **Contract templates and terms** referenced in Agent 10 (Legal & IP) require
  lawyer review before use. Contract law varies by jurisdiction.
- **POSH/ICC procedures** in Agent 11 follow the Indian POSH Act 2013 framework
  but must be implemented with guidance from a practicing lawyer and trained
  ICC members.
- **IP strategy** (trademarks, patents, copyright) requires professional IP
  counsel. Filing errors can be costly and irreversible.

### Financial

The financial models, unit economics frameworks, fundraising guidance, and salary
bands in this repository (Agent 18, compensation-bands.md, founders-playbook.md)
are **illustrative frameworks, not financial advice.**

Specifically:
- **Salary bands** are approximate market ranges as of early 2026 and will become
  outdated. Always benchmark against current market data before making offers.
- **Financial projections** are templates. Your actual numbers depend on your
  specific business, market, and circumstances.
- **Tax guidance** (Section 80-IAC, QSBS, GST, etc.) is general information.
  Consult a chartered accountant (India) or CPA/tax attorney (US) for your
  specific situation.
- **Fundraising guidance** is based on common patterns, not guarantees. Every
  fundraise is unique.

### Security

The security frameworks in Agent 09 provide a structured approach to security
planning but **do not replace professional security assessment.**

Specifically:
- **Penetration testing** must be performed by qualified security professionals,
  not self-assessed using a checklist.
- **The breach response playbook** is a starting framework. Your actual incident
  response plan must be tested through tabletop exercises with your security team.
- **PCI-DSS compliance** requires formal assessment by a Qualified Security
  Assessor (QSA) for most merchants. A checklist is not compliance.
- **Security architecture** recommendations should be reviewed by a security
  engineer familiar with your specific tech stack.

### HR & Employment

Employment law varies dramatically by jurisdiction. The HR frameworks in Agent 22
and compensation-bands.md **must be adapted to your local employment laws.**

Specifically:
- **Employment contracts, termination procedures, and severance calculations**
  must comply with local labor law. What's legal in one jurisdiction may be
  illegal in another.
- **POSH committee requirements** (India), **at-will employment** (US),
  **notice periods** (EU/UK) all have jurisdiction-specific rules that override
  general guidance in this repository.
- Always consult an employment lawyer before terminating an employee,
  implementing a PIP, or making significant changes to compensation.

### Healthcare / Medical

If any product built using this framework involves health data or health advice:
- Comply with HIPAA (US), ABDM (India), GDPR special categories (EU)
- Medical disclaimers are mandatory on every health-related feature
- Clinical validation may be required before making health claims
- Consult healthcare regulatory counsel for your specific product

## Human Involvement Requirements

Many processes described in this repository **require human judgment and cannot
be fully automated.** The system identifies automation opportunities but the
following areas specifically require qualified human involvement:

| Area | Why Humans Are Required |
|------|----------------------|
| Legal review | Contracts, policies, and compliance assessments require lawyer judgment |
| Financial audit | Statutory audits require qualified auditors (CA/CPA) |
| HR decisions | Hiring, termination, and PIP decisions involve human rights and legal risk |
| Security assessment | Penetration testing requires certified professionals |
| Board governance | Board decisions require human directors with fiduciary duties |
| Crisis management | Crisis response requires real-time human judgment |
| Content moderation | Edge cases in trust & safety require human judgment |
| Medical/health | Health-related decisions require licensed professionals |
| Tax filing | Tax returns require qualified tax professionals |
| Insurance | Coverage decisions require licensed insurance professionals |
| Regulatory filings | SEC/SEBI/regulatory filings require qualified professionals |
| Dispute resolution | Legal disputes require qualified mediators/arbitrators |
| Investigation | Whistleblower and harassment investigations require trained investigators |

**The SOPs and process maps in this repository flag automation opportunities (⚡)
but also explicitly mark steps that require human review. Both are by design.**
